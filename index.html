About

Hi my name is Lisa Wehrs. Throughout my 10 years of working with Alzheimer’s and Dementia, I have found that this is the path for me. I have planned the opening of Take a Break Respite Care for 2 years now and feel very excited that I can offer my services to the families who need them. I have a passion for taking care of those who have these diseases. I also want to help you as caregivers, those of you who dedicate your valuable time trying to create quality in your loved ones life. Together, we can make this happen. Take a Break Respite Care is dedicated to only those with Alzheimer’s and Dementia, creating a welcoming atmosphere with activities geared toward each individual’s needs. Caregiving is a full time commitment, give yourself and your loved one some rest and join us at Take a Break Respite Care. Our trained staff would love to help make this fulfillment happen.

Sincerely,

Lisa Wehrs

What We Offer

  • A facility catering only to Alzheimer’s and Dementia
  • Activities geared towards the different stages of Alzheimer’s for each individual’s needs
  • All staff are CPR/First Aid certified
  • Flexible Hours
  • We can help locate outside sources if needed
  • We are a member of MADSA (Michigan Adult Day Services)
  • Offer health conscious lunches and snacks
  • Low staff to resident ratio
  • Services such as barber services, photographers, nurse, church singing groups etc., offered at discounted prices and free if applicable
  • Smaller facility with more of a personal feel
  • Create a weekly schedule or at least a 48 hour notice for a guaranteed opening or call last minute and if we are staffed you are more than welcome to come on in.
  • Call last minute and if we are staffed and under capacity, you are more than welcome to come on in.

Requirements

  • Center Typically designed for persons 55 years of age and older. However, special consideration is given to those under the age of 55 whose needs may be met in an adult day program setting.
  • Must be diagnosed with Alzheimer’s or Dementia of any sort
  • Must be able to bare weight or semi ambulatory
  • No tube feedings or persons who need injections unless other arrangement can be made
  • All medications & equipment for administering must be provided
