About

Hi my name is Lisa Wehrs. Throughout my 10 years of working with Alzheimer’s and Dementia, I have found that this is the path for me. I have planned the opening of Take a Break Respite Care for 2 years now and feel very excited that I can finally offer my services to those who need them. Not only do I have a passion for taking care of those who have these diseases, I also want to help you as caregivers. Those of you who dedicate everyday of your lives trying to make your loved ones life still as fulfilling as it was before. Together, we can make this happen. Take a Break Respite Care is dedicated to only those with Alzheimer’s and Dementia, creating a welcoming atmosphere with activities geared to each individual’s needs. Caregiving is a full time commitment, give yourself and your loved one some rest and join us at Take a Break Respite Care. Our trained staff would love to help make the fulfillment happen.

Sincerely,

Lisa Wehrs
